About Me

Hi there, I'm James. Through photography I've come to learn patience and appreciation for fine details. I always want my photos to imagine a bold, exciting world - one in which my subject stands out and shines in the most genuine manner. FACES is a peak into this process by which I bring the creative brief to life and make the subject in my photographs part of a story that they would like to tell.
